Our Features

Online Consultations

Provide secure video consultations to patients and manage appointments efficiently.

Medicine Cross-Referencing

Cross-check medicines prescribed in different countries to find equivalent drugs, banned substances, and alternative medications worldwide.

Medical Updates

Stay updated with the latest verified medical research and guidelines.

Request for health camps

Doctors can easily request health camps in areas they believe need urgent medical attention. Whether it’s a rural village or an underserved community, MEDTrust connects your request with active NGOs, helping you bring care where it’s needed most.
