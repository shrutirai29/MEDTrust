Our Features

Connect with Doctors for Health Camps

Helps match NGOs with verified healthcare professionals for specific medical needs. This ensures better outreach and quality medical support in underserved areas.

Locate Villages for Health Camps

Use local health data to find places where people need medical help the most. This helps NGOs plan camps where they are really needed, so no resources are wasted. NGOs can also get requests from doctors or people in the community and set up camps in those areas based on the demand.

Real-time Health Camp Management

Provides live updates on camp attendance, doctor availability, and medical supply needs. Enables better coordination between volunteers, doctors, and organizers.

Access to Verified Medical Insights

get access to trusted medical guidelines and resources for better healthcare delivery. Helps in educating volunteers and patients with accurate health information.
